About

TeenHustle is a youth empowerment platform built to tackle teenage unemployment by giving Nigerian teens access to safe micro-jobs, practical skills, and real digital work experience. Instead of waiting until after school or graduation, TeenHustle allows young people to start building value, confidence, and income early.
Through guided tasks, learning paths, and reward-based challenges, teens can grow their skills while earning in a secure and supportive environment. The platform is designed to help teenagers discover their strengths, gain work experience, and prepare for the future—whether that means higher education, entrepreneurship, or a career in technology.

Our Vission

To become the leading youth-powered work platform in Africa, where teenagers grow into skilled, confident, and financially independent adults. We envision a future where young people are equipped early with the tools, experience, and mindset needed to thrive in a digital and competitive world.

Our Mission

To empower Nigerian teenagers by providing access to safe and practical work opportunities, digital skills, and early financial independence. TeenHustle is committed to helping young people gain real experience, build confidence, and develop skills that prepare them for future careers and entrepreneurship.
